Atoms:

An atom is a building block of the universe made out of 3 things. Protons, Neutrons, and Electrons. Protons and Neutrons make up
the nucleus, which can be called the center of the atom, and electrons orbit it on a path called a shell. Protons and neutrons are made
up of quarks. A quark is another particle, and there are 6 types of quarks, top quark, bottom quark, string quark, strange quark
, charm quark and left quark, which are glued together by gluons. Protons and neutrons are made of top and bottom quarks.
Sometimes top and bottom quarks are called up and down quarks.

of the particles and forces.) The mass of it is 125.09±0.21 (stat.)±0.11 (syst.) GeV/c² (CMS+ATLAS). The electric charge is 0 e.
The mean life time might be 1.56x10-22s. it was discovered in 2011 to 2013.it’s symbol looks like a H. Made by the quantum excitation

by the Higgs boson, which is used by the field to continuously interact with other particles, like the electron.
