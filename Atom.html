Atoms:

An atom is a building block of the universe and is made out of 3 things.
   - Protons
   - Neutrons
   - Electrons

Protons and Neutrons make up the nucleus ,which can be called the center of the atom, and electrons orbit it on a path called a shell. Protons and neutrons are made up of quarks. A quark is another particle and there are 6 types of quarks.
   - Top quark
   - Bottom quark
   - String quark
   - Strange quark
   - Charm quark and
   - Left quark
Those quarks are glued together by gluons. Protons and neutrons are made of top and bottom quarks. Sometimes top and bottom quarks are called up and down quarks.

Higgs Boson:

The higgs boson is a VERY important particle. It's the only reason that particles have mass, and why they can from into atoms and stuff like that. But what is a higgs boson? (the higgs boson is in the Standard Model. The Standard Model gives a framework for our current understanding of the particles and forces.)
The mass of it is 125.09±0.21 (stat.)±0.11 (syst.) GeV/c² (CMS+ATLAS).
The electric charge is 0 e. The mean life time might be 1.56x10-22s. it was discovered in 2011 to 2013. It's symbol looks like a H and made by the quantum excitation of the Higgs field. The Higgs field is a field of energy that is thought to be in every region of the universe. The field is accompanied by the Higgs boson, which is used by the field to continuously interact with other particles, like the electron.
